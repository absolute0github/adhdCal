## Premium Feature Ideas

### Scheduling & Calendar

| Feature | Description | ADHD Benefit |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **Multiple Calendar Support** | Connect Google, Outlook, Apple calendars | See everything in one place, reduce context switching |
| **Extended Scheduling Window** | Schedule up to 30 days ahead | Better long-term planning without overwhelm |
| **Smart Time Blocking** | AI suggests optimal work blocks based on your patterns | Reduces decision fatigue |
| **Buffer Time** | Auto-add travel/transition time between events | Prevents back-to-back overwhelm |
| **Energy Matching** | Tag tasks by energy level, schedule during your peak hours | Work with your brain, not against it |

## Monetization Notes

### Conversion Strategy
- Free tier should be genuinely useful (not crippled)
- Premium removes friction, doesn't gatekeep core functionality
- Trial: 14-day free premium trial for new users

### Pricing Psychology
- Monthly: $5.99 (low barrier)
- Annual: $49.99 (save ~30%, encourage commitment)
- Consider: "Pay what you can" option for students/low-income

### ADHD-Specific Considerations
- Simple, clear pricing (no confusing tiers)
- Easy cancellation (reduces signup anxiety)
- Pause subscription option (for overwhelm periods)
